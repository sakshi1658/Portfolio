Product Designer & Requirement Analyst

Skills Used

  • Information Architecture

Overview

The NAMAN Mental Health Program is a government initiative developed with NIMHANS and AHT, addressing rural mental health challenges. I helped design the CHW Screening Module to streamline screenings, consent, and referrals through a tablet-based app used by frontline health workers.

Key Features

  • Seamless integration with ration card & KUDUMBA Survey databases
  • Consent management with privacy and ethics
  • MERIT-based mental health screening
  • Appointment scheduling at PHCs

Software Used

Figma, Adobe Illustrator, Axure RP
